ASTRA CUSTOM VISUAL SCANNER BUILD
Version: 20260522-custom-visual-scanner-v1

What changed:
- The scanner now keeps the existing QR scan support.
- The scanner also recognises the six ASTRA anomaly images directly using app-only visual target recognition.
- The six printable scan target PNGs are included in:
  assets/printable-anomaly-scan-targets/

Important:
- These are not normal QR codes.
- A normal phone camera will not know what they are.
- The ASTRA app recognises the image target/signature and maps it to the correct anomaly marker:
  ASTRA:UKGE:ANOMALY-01 through ASTRA:UKGE:ANOMALY-06.
- For best real-world scanning, print the image clearly and aim the scanner so the anomaly image fills most of the scanner view.
