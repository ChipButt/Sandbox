ASTRA Custom Visual Scanner v3 - Image Lock Fix

Reason for rebuild:
- v2 was too strict and too dependent on a centred, perfect full-frame image.
- The printable targets also had too much empty black space, making real camera scanning from a screen/poster unreliable.

Changes in v3:
- Rebuilt the six printable anomaly targets so the anomaly fills the frame more closely.
- Added small integrated ASTRA constellation glyphs. They are not QR codes or barcodes and should look like anomaly detail, but they give the scanner a real repeatable pattern to lock onto.
- Scanner now detects object/content regions in the camera frame before comparing pattern fingerprints.
- Scanner now compares against multiple camera/screen variants of each target.
- Scanner requires two matching frames before unlocking.
- QR fallback is still present.
- Colour-only triggering remains removed.

Test guidance:
- Display or print the v3 target images, not the older v1/v2 target images.
- Hold the target so the anomaly image fills roughly half to most of the scanner view.
- Avoid glare/reflections if scanning from a monitor.
