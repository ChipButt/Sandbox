# Astra Field Terminal

Open `index.html` to test locally. For camera scanning, host the folder on HTTPS (GitHub Pages, Netlify, etc.) because browsers usually block camera access from local files.

## QR codes
Printable QR codes are in `/qrcodes`.

The app accepts these payloads:

- `ASTRA-HQ-AZUREBRINE` — Astra HQ / Roll Britannia
- `ASTRA-VENUE-2-CINDERCAP` — Venue 2
- `ASTRA-VENUE-3-ECHOSHELL` — Venue 3
- `ASTRA-VENUE-4-GLIMMERROOT` — Venue 4
- `ASTRA-VENUE-5-HOLLOWCLAW` — Venue 5
- `ASTRA-VENUE-6-NULLGLASS` — Venue 6

## Reward logic

- 1 unique scan: Common
- 2 unique scans: Uncommon
- 3 unique scans: Rare
- 4 unique scans: Very Rare
- 5 unique scans: Legendary — gift claim available
- 6 unique scans: Godlike — gift claim + prize draw entry
